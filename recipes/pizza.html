Pizza Recipe


Ingredients:


  • Bread Flour - 700g
  • All-Purpose Flour - 300g
  • Water (cold) - 650g
  • Salt - 25g
  • Sugar - 20g
  • Olive Oil - 20g
  • Instant Yeast - .3g (4 pinches)


Steps:


  1. Mix Dry Ingredients
  2. Add Cold Water
  3. Rest (Autolyse)
  4. Fold The Dough (Bench method)
  5. Bulk Ferment
  6. Divide and Ball
  7. Final Rest
