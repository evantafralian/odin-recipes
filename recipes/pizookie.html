Pizookie Recipe


Ingredients:


  • All-Purpose Flour - 1 cup
  • Brown Sugar - 1/2 cup
  • Egg - 1 egg
  • Baking Soda - 1/5 tsp
  • Chocolate chips - 3/4 cup
  • Salt - 1/4 tsp


Steps:


  1. Mix Wet Ingredients
  2. Add Dry Ingredients
  3. Fold in chocolate
  4. Mix
  5. Let sit in fridge
  6. Divide and Ball
  7. Oven for 20 Minutes
