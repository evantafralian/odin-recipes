Moussaka Recipe


Ingredients:

  • Ground beef - 500g
  • Potatoes - 6 whole (sliced)
  • Egg - 5 eggs
  • Avocado Oil - 20g
  • Pepper - 1/4 tsp
  • Salt - 1/4 tsp


Steps:

  1. Slice potatoes
  2. Cook Ground Beef
  3. Oil pan, preheat oven
  4. Layer potatoes, then beef, and so on
  5. Heat in oven - 400 degrees Fairenheit
  6. Cook for 45 Minutes
  7. Put in Eggs last 15 Minutes
